
    iGaming CMS Installation Instructions
    
    Step 1

    Upload the contents of the zip file somewhere onto your web server.



    Step 2

    Open your web browser and go to the /install directory where you uploaded iGaming.



    Step 3

    Follow the installation instructions and fill in all required fields.
